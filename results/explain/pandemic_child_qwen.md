# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

Sure! Let me explain the rules in a simple way that's easy for a 10-year-old to understand:

---

Hey there, little explorer! We're going on an exciting journey where we fight against some very bad germs. You and your friends will be special doctors working together to save the world from these diseases.

### How to Start the Game:
1. **Divide Cards**: Split the cards into piles based on how hard you want the game to be.
2. **Give Roles**: Each player gets a card that tells them what superpower they have, like being a scientist or an operations expert.
3. **Set Up Board**: Place the board in the middle of the table and put some special stations called "Research Stations" on it.

### Taking Turns:
1. **4 Actions per Turn**: You get to do 4 things each turn.
2. **Draw Cards**: After doing your actions, you draw 2 new cards from a pile.
3. **Be the Infector**: The player who just finished their turn gets to play some cards that spread germs around the world.

### Your Actions:
1. **Move Around**: You can use special cards to fly from one city to another or build Research Stations which help you move faster.
2. **Treat Diseases**: If a disease is in your city, you can remove it by using a card with the same color of the disease.
3. **Discover Cures**: Collect 4-5 cards of the same color and use them at a Research Station to find a cure for that disease!
4. **Share Knowledge**: Pass your valuable cards to help your friends discover cures faster.

### Winning or Losing:
1. **Win by Finding Cures**: If you and your team can find cures for all 4 diseases, you win! It's like solving a big puzzle together.
2. **Lose if Outbreaks Happen Too Much**: If cities get too many germs (more than 3 of the same color), they outbreak and spread to nearby cities. Keep an eye out so this doesn’t happen!
3. **Epidemics Make Things Harder**: Sometimes, a special card will make it harder for everyone by spreading lots of germs quickly.

### Working Together:
Remember, you’re all on the same team! Share your cards and ideas with each other to beat the diseases faster. It’s like playing as one big superhero!

---

That's how you play Pandemic! It's a fun game where you work together to save the world from dangerous germs. Good luck, brave heroes!
