# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

**Welcome to Pandemic, a fun game where you work together to save the world from deadly diseases!**

**Here are the simple rules:**

1. **Setup:** We have four colors of disease cubes (red, blue, yellow, black) and special cards for infections (Epidemics), outbreaks, and special events. You'll also see pawns for players, a board with cities around the world, cure markers, infection rate track, outbreak indicator, player discard pile, infection discard pile, player draw pile, infection draw pile, reference cards, role cards, and your own hand of player cards.

2. **Your Role:** Each player has a special role like being a Scientist (finds cures faster), Medic (treats diseases better), or Operations Expert (builds research stations quickly). You'll use these skills to fight the diseases together!

3. **Game Flow:**
   - Players take turns doing actions, fighting diseases, and helping each other.
   - On your turn, you do four things in this order:
     1. **Take Action:** Move around the world, treat diseases, build research stations, share cards with others, or discover cures (if you're at a research station).
     2. **Draw Cards:** Pick two more player cards from the pile to add to your hand.
     3. **Play as the Infector:** Draw infection cards equal to the current infection rate, and place disease cubes on the cities shown. If there are already three cubes of one color in a city, it outbreaks, spreading the disease to nearby cities!
     4. **Check for Win or Lose Conditions:**
         - You win if all four diseases are cured (cure markers show vial-side up).
         - You lose if any of these happen:
           - There aren't enough disease cubes left when you need to infect.
           - The eighth outbreak occurs, moving the outbreaks marker to the skull symbol.
           - When drawing cards, there aren't any left in the player draw pile.

4. **Epidemics:** Sometimes you'll draw an Epidemic card! This makes things harder:
   - Move the infection rate up one space on the track.
   - Shuffle and place the infection discard pile back on top of the infection draw pile.
   - Draw the bottom card from the infection draw pile, add three cubes to that city, then place the card into the infection discard pile. Outbreaks happen if there are already three cubes of one color!

5. **Special Events:** Some player cards have special powers! You can play these at any time during your turn or even on another player's turn, following the instructions on the card.

6. **Helping Each Other:** Remember, you're all on the same team fighting diseases! You can move others' pawns with a Charter Flight action, treat diseases in other cities (spending an extra action), share cards with others using the Share Knowledge action, or build research stations together!

**Now that you know the rules, let's save the world from those nasty diseases! Have fun playing Pandemic together!**
