# Rule Explanation for Adult Audience

Certainly! Here's a clear and concise explanation of the rules for "Century," focusing on setup, gameplay, and winning conditions:

### Setup

1. **Shuffle and Draw Point Cards (A):**
   - Shuffle the 20 orange-backed Point cards to form a deck.
   - Place 5 face-up Point cards in a row to the left of this deck.

2. **Place Tokens:**
   - Above the first (leftmost) Point card, place a pile of gold tokens equal to twice the number of players.
   - Above the second Point card, place a pile of silver tokens also equal to twice the number of players.

3. **Set Up Merchant Cards (E):**
   - Among the 10 purple-bordered Merchant cards, each player gets one 'Create 2' and one 'Upgrade 2'.
   - The remaining starting Merchant cards are returned to the box.
   - Shuffle the rest of the Merchant cards to form a deck.

4. **Draw Face-Up Merchant Cards (F):**
   - Draw 6 face-up Merchant cards from the deck and place them in a row to the left of it.

5. **Prepare Spice Cubes:**
   - Separate cubes by color and place them into the bowls according to the order shown: Yellow (Turmeric), Red (Saffron), Green (Cardamom), Brown (Cinnamon).

6. **Deal Caravan Cards:**
   - Each player takes a Caravan card, ensuring one has the first-player symbol.
   - Shuffle these cards and deal each player one in front of them.

7. **Place Yellow and Red Cubes on Caravans:**
   - Place cubes according to the table provided:
     - 1st player: 3 yellow
     - 2nd and 3rd players: 4 yellow
     - 4th and 5th players: 3 yellow, 1 red

### Gameplay

1. **Turn Sequence:**
   - Each round, every player takes one turn in clockwise order.
   - Actions on a turn are:
     - Play: Place a card face up to execute its effect.
     - Acquire: Take a Merchant card by placing cubes and then add them to your caravan.
     - Rest: Return played cards back to your hand.
     - Claim: Use your cubes to claim Point cards.

2. **Card Effects (Merchant Cards):**
   - **Spice Cards:** Place the shown number of cubes on your Caravan.
   - **Upgrade Cards:** Upgrade up to two cubes one level at a time.
   - **Trade Cards:** Exchange cubes between your Caravan and the supply bowls as many times as you can.

3. **Acquiring Merchant Cards:**
   - Pay by placing any cube from your caravan on each card to the left of the card you want.
   - Place the acquired card in your hand, and fill the empty slot with a new card drawn from the deck.

4. **Resting:**
   - Return all played cards back into your hand for future turns (deck-building).

5. **Claiming Point Cards:**
   - Use cubes matching those on the Point card.
   - Place claimed cards face down and take any tokens due to claiming a Point card.

### End of Game

1. **End Conditions:**
   - The game ends when a player has taken their fifth (or sixth in 2-3 player games) Point card.
   
2. **Scoring:**
   - Count points from all Point cards, gold/silver tokens, and remaining cubes on your Caravan:
     - Each gold token = 3 points
     - Each silver token = 1 point
     - Each non-yellow cube = 1 point

3. **Determine Winner:**
   - The player with the most points wins.
   - In a tie, the last player to take their turn wins.

### Credits and Final Notes:
- Century is designed by Emerson Matsuuchi and produced by Sophie Gravel.
- It involves strategic card play, resource management, and point collection through trading and caravan upgrades.
- Enjoy your journey across the Spice Road!

This setup ensures a clear understanding of how to begin the game, while the gameplay rules detail the actions players can take each turn. The end-of-game scoring system provides a concrete way to determine the winner, ensuring an engaging experience for adult players.
