# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

Sure! Let's break down the game Century into simple steps that a 10-year-old can understand:

### Setting Up the Game

1. **Shuffle the Point Cards**: Think of these as treasure cards. We mix them up and put five face-up in a line.

2. **Gold and Silver Tokens**: For each player, we place two gold tokens above the first point card and two silver tokens above the second one. These are like coins that you can earn!

3. **Starting Cards for Merchants**: There are special cards called Merchant cards. We lay out ten of these face-up. Each player gets a 'Create 2' and an 'Upgrade 2' card to start.

4. **Spice Cubes**: These are colorful little cubes (yellow, red, green, brown) that you will use on your caravan. We separate them by color and put them in bowls.

5. **Caravan Cards**: Each player gets a Caravan card with some yellow and red cubes already placed on it. The player who has the first player symbol goes first.

### Taking Turns

- **Each Player Takes One Turn Per Round**: You go around the circle, starting from the first player.
  
- **What Can You Do?**:
  - **Play a Card**: Pick one of your cards and place it in front of you. If it's a Spice card, put cubes on your caravan based on what the card says.
  - **Acquire a Merchant Card**: Place any cube from your caravan on each card to the left of the one you want. You can get free cards if you don’t need to pay with cubes!
  - **Rest**: Put all played cards back into your hand. This helps you plan better for future turns.
  - **Claim a Point Card**: If you have all the right cubes, take a point card and put it in front of you. You get extra coins if you claim certain cards first!

### How to Play Cards

- **Spice Cards**: Pick a Spice card and place the colored cubes shown on your caravan.
  
- **Upgrade Cards**: You can make some of your yellow cubes turn into red, then maybe even green ones. It’s like upgrading your toys!
  
- **Trade Cards**: Take cubes from your caravan and get new ones in return. You can do this as many times as you want if you have enough cubes.

### Game End

The game ends when a player gets their fifth (or sixth in smaller games) point card. Everyone counts their points, coins, and remaining cubes on their Caravan cards. The person with the most points wins! If there’s a tie, the last player to take their turn wins.

That's it! Century is all about trading spices, upgrading your caravan, and collecting points. Have fun exploring the Spice Road!
