# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

Hey there, kiddo! Today, we're going to learn about a super cool board game called Wingspan!

**The Goal:** Your goal is to collect as many birds as possible and put them in your aviary (that's like a big bird house!). The player with the most points at the end of the game wins!

**How to Play:**

1. First, we need to set up the game board. This will show us where all the different habitats are.
2. Each player gets their own special mat and some food tokens (these are like little eggs that help you collect birds).
3. The game is divided into four rounds. In each round, players take turns doing one of four things:
	* Playing a bird from your hand onto the board
	* Getting food tokens by rolling dice
	* Laying eggs on your birds
	* Drawing new cards to add to your collection
4. When you play a bird, you need to pay attention to its food cost and egg requirements. Don't worry, it's like solving a puzzle!
5. Some birds have special powers that can help or hurt you. It's like having a superpower!
6. At the end of each round, we score points for all the birds and eggs on our board.
7. The game ends after four rounds, and then we count up all our points to see who won!

**Some Important Rules:**

* You can only play one bird per turn.
* If you have a bird with an egg requirement, you need to pay that many food tokens to lay the egg.
* Some birds are harder to get than others because they require specific food types (like fish or insects).
* Don't worry if you run out of food tokens – you can cache them (save them) for later!

**Tips and Tricks:**

* Try to collect a variety of birds with different habitats and powers.
* Pay attention to the dice rolls and try to get the right food tokens for your birds.
* Use your bonus cards wisely – they can give you an extra boost!
* Don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from other players.

That's it! Wingspan is a fun game that's all about collecting and caring for birds. Are you ready to start playing?
