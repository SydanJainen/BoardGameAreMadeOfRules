# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

**Welcome to Wingspan! Here's how you play:**

1. **Setup:**
   - Each player gets a mat, action cubes, bonus cards, bird cards, and food tokens.
   - Choose 5 bird cards to keep (discard others) and pay for them with your food tokens.
   - Choose 1 bonus card to keep.
   - Place the goal board on the table, showing spaces for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place goals.

2. **Gameplay:**
   On your turn, you can do one of these things:
   - **Play a Bird:** Put an action cube at the top of where you want to play the bird. Pay food and egg costs, then place the bird on your mat.
   - **Gain Food:** Put an action cube in the leftmost slot of the "gain food" row. Take food tokens from dice in the birdfeeder. You can also discard a bird card for extra food.
   - **Lay Eggs:** Put an action cube in the leftmost slot of the "lay eggs" row. Lay eggs on birds according to their egg limit.
   - **Draw Bird Cards:** Put an action cube in the leftmost slot of the "draw cards" row. Draw cards from the face-up bird tray or deck.

3. **Bird Powers:**
   Some birds have special powers that you can use when you play them, activate them (with brown powers), or once between turns (with pink powers). Follow what the card says to use these powers.

4. **End of Round:**
   - Remove all action cubes from your mat.
   - Score end-of-round goals: count how many birds or eggs you have in a given habitat or nest type, then place an action cube on the goal board based on your rank order.
   - Discard face-up bird cards and replenish them with 3 new ones.
   - Rotate the first player token clockwise to the next player.

5. **Game End:**
   The game ends after Round 4. Score points for each bird card, bonus card, end-of-round goals, eggs, cached food, and tucked birds. The player with the most points wins!

**Fun Examples:**
- If you want to play a bird in column 2 or 3, you must discard 1 egg to the egg supply.
- Some birds can tuck other cards under them to represent creating a flock.
- You can cache food tokens on bird cards until the end of the game. This means you save that food token and it's worth 1 point at the end of the game.

Have fun playing Wingspan! Remember, it's okay if you don't understand everything right away – just keep trying your best!
