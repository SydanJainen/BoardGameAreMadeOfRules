# Rule Explanation for Adult Audience

**Wingspan: A Bird-Collecting Engine Builder**

**Objective:** Accumulate points by attracting birds to your aviary, gaining food tokens via dice, laying eggs, drawing bird cards, activating bird powers, scoring bonus cards, and achieving end-of-round goals. The player with the most points at the game's end wins.

**Components:**
1. Rulebook
2. Goal board (green or blue side)
3. Bird tray & 170 bird cards
4. Bonus card deck & 26 bonus cards
5. Egg miniatures (75 pieces)
6. Custom wooden dice (5 pieces)
7. Wooden action cubes (8 each for up to 5 players)
8. Food tokens (40 pieces in five types)
9. Goal tiles (eight pieces)
10. First player token & scorepad

**Setup:**
- Shuffle bird cards and place three face-up on the tray.
- Create food, egg, and action cube supplies.
- Choose a goal board side (green or blue).
- Place four random goal tiles on the board.
- Distribute bonus cards to players (two each, keep one).
- Deal five bird cards and five food tokens to each player.
- Keep up to five bird cards, discarding excess food tokens.
- Randomly select the first player.

**Gameplay:**
Wingspan is played over four rounds. Each round consists of turns taken clockwise until all players have placed their eight action cubes. On your turn:

1. **Play a Bird:** Place an action cube at the top of the desired habitat column, pay food and egg costs (if any), and place the bird card on your mat. Activate its "WHEN PLAYED" power.

2. **Gain Food:** Place an action cube in the leftmost exposed slot in the gain food row, select dice from the birdfeeder to gain matching food tokens, optionally discard a bird card for extra food, and activate forest birds' brown powers (from right to left).

3. **Lay Eggs:** Place an action cube in the lay eggs row's leftmost slot, gain egg tokens equal to the number of eggs laid, optionally pay one food token for additional eggs, and activate grassland birds' brown powers.

4. **Draw Bird Cards:** Place an action cube in the draw cards row's leftmost slot, draw cards from either the face-up bird tray or deck (up to three), optionally discard an egg for extra card, and activate wetland birds' brown powers.

**Action Cube Movement:** After completing one of the four actions, move your action cube along its row, activating brown powers as you go. Once it reaches the far left, end your turn.

**Bird Powers:**
- Brown ("WHEN ACTIVATED"): Activate during turns using corresponding habitats.
- Pink ("ONCE BETWEEN TURNS"): Trigger on opponents' turns once between each of your own turns.
- "WHEN PLAYED": Activate only when playing a bird (never again).

**End-of-Round Goals:**
At round's end, score goals based on birds/eggs in habitats/nest types. Place action cubes on the goal board according to scoring method: green (majority) or blue (one point per item). Remove all action cubes from your mat.

**Game End & Scoring:**
The game ends after completing Round 4. Score points for:
- Bird cards' printed values
- Bonus cards' printed values
- Eggs on bird cards
- Cached food tokens
- Tucked bird cards
- End-of-round goals

The player with the most points wins. In case of a tie, the player with more unused food tokens wins. If still tied, share the victory.

**Additional Rules:**
- Managing the birdfeeder & deck.
- Bird powers details (in Appendix).
- Scoring bonus cards (in Appendix).

Enjoy your game of Wingspan!
