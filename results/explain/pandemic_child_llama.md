# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

Hey kids! Today we're going to talk about a super cool game called Pandemic. It's all about working together with your friends to save the world from four bad diseases!

Here's how you play:

**Step 1: Choose Your Job**
Each player chooses a different job, like a Scientist, Operations Expert, or Medic. Each job has its own special powers that will help us solve the puzzle.

**Step 2: Move Around the World**
We all move our pawns around the world to different cities where diseases are spreading. We have to work together to stop the diseases from spreading!

**Step 3: Draw Cards**
When it's my turn, I draw a card that tells me what disease is spreading in which city. If I'm in that city, I have to do something about it.

**Step 4: Solve the Puzzle**
Depending on my job and the disease, I might need to:

* Move to a different city to find resources
* Give medicine to people who are sick
* Build a research station to find a cure
* Travel around the world to meet other players

We all work together to solve the puzzle. If we're not careful, the diseases will spread too far and... **DEFEAT!**

**Step 5: Find Cures**
If we succeed in stopping the diseases from spreading, we get to find cures for each one! The first player to find a cure wins!

Here are some important rules:

* We can only move our pawns around if there's a disease in that city.
* If a city has too many cubes of the same color (that's what we call "infection"), it will have an **OUTBREAK**. This is when more people get sick!
* We can't put too many cubes in one city, or it will be too infected and we'll lose.

That's it! It might seem hard at first, but with teamwork and strategy, we can save the world from those bad diseases!

Are you ready to join the Pandemic team?
