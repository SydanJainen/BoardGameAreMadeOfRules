# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

Sure! Let's break it down into simple steps that a 10-year-old can understand:

### Setting Up the Game

1. **Get Your Tools Ready**:
   - You have lots of pretty tiles with pictures of nature (like mountains, forests) and little animal tokens (like bears, elk).
   - There’s also a cloth bag for the animals.

2. **Pick Tiles**:
   - Shuffle all the habitat tiles well.
   - Each player gets 5 special tiles called "starter" tiles to begin with.

3. **Choose Wildlife Tokens**:
   - Put all the animal tokens in a cloth bag and shake them up!
   - Draw some of these tokens (20 for each type: bear, elk, salmon, hawk, fox).

4. **Place Tiles on the Table**:
   - Put 4 habitat tiles face-up on the table.
   - Pair each token with one of those tiles.

### Playing the Game

1. **Start Playing**:
   - The person who saw a wildlife animal in real life first gets to go first.
   - Players take turns adding their own tiles and tokens.

2. **Pick Tiles and Tokens**:
   - Each turn, you pick a tile and a token from the table.
   - You can also use special nature tokens to get extra choices or wipe away too many of one type of animal.

3. **Place Your Tile and Token**:
   - Place your new habitat tile next to an existing one (make sure it touches).
   - Put the animal token on a tile that matches its picture.
   - If you can’t place an animal, put the token back in the bag.

4. **Replace Used Tiles and Tokens**:
   - Take the used tiles and tokens out of play.
   - Replace them with new ones from the stack or bag.

### Winning the Game

1. **Keep Playing**:
   - You keep playing until there are no more habitat tiles left to replace.
   - Everyone gets 20 turns in total.

2. **Score at the End**:
   - Count your points by looking at how many animals and habitats you have.
   - Look for groups of animals or big areas with one type of habitat.
   - Nature tokens are worth extra points too!

### Fun Facts about Cascadia

- The game is based on real places in the Pacific Northwest, like forests, rivers, and mountains.
- You can learn about cool animals like grizzly bears, elk, salmon, hawks, and foxes while playing.

That's it! Just remember to have fun building your beautiful landscape with nature tiles and animal tokens. Happy gaming!
