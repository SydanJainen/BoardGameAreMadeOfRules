# Rule Explanation for Expert Audience

Pandemic is a cooperative board game designed by Matt Leacock, where players take on the roles of disease-fighting specialists working together to stop the spread of four deadly diseases across the globe. The game requires strategic planning, teamwork, and communication among players to achieve its objective.

**Objective:**
The ultimate goal of the game is to find cures for all four diseases before they overtake the world, thereby saving humanity from extinction.

**Gameplay Overview:**

1. **Initial Setup:** Each player selects a role (e.g., Scientist, Operations Expert, Medic) and receives a unique set of abilities and skills.
2. **Turn Structure:** A game turn consists of three phases:
	* **Action Phase:** Players take individual turns performing one action each, such as moving to a new location, treating an infected city, or researching a cure.
	* **Research Phase:** Players can conduct research to develop cures for the diseases.
	* **Movement Phase:** Players move their pawns around the board to reach new locations and respond to outbreaks.
3. **Infection Mechanic:** When a player lands on a location with an outbreak, they must draw an Infection card, which determines the number of cubes (representing infected people) added to that location.
4. **Cure Development:** Players can develop cures by researching specific diseases. Each disease requires a certain number of research tokens, and players must collect these tokens to unlock the cure.
5. **Epidemic Cards:** When an Epidemic card is drawn, it triggers an outbreak in one or more cities, increasing the Infection Rate Track on the board. Players must respond to this increased threat by adding cubes to affected locations.

**Outbreak Mechanic:**
When a city reaches three infected cubes of the same color (e.g., all blue), an outbreak occurs, triggering additional outbreaks in adjacent cities. This chain reaction can spread rapidly and require players to act quickly to prevent global catastrophe.

**Game End Conditions:**

1. **Victory:** Players collectively win the game when they discover cures for all four diseases.
2. **Defeat:** The game ends immediately if:
	* There are not enough cubes in the supply to add to the board during an Epidemic.
	* A city reaches three infected cubes of the same color, triggering an outbreak.
	* The Outbreaks Marker reaches the skull symbol on the Outbreak Indicator (indicating eight outbreaks have occurred).
3. **Chain Reactions:** If a new cube would cause the total number of cubes in an adjacent city to exceed three, additional outbreaks may occur, leading to a chain reaction.

**Key Strategies:**

1. **Cooperative Play:** Players must work together, sharing information and resources, to achieve their objective.
2. **Resource Management:** Players must manage their research tokens, movement capabilities, and action points effectively to respond to outbreaks and develop cures.
3. **Infection Rate Management:** Players must monitor the Infection Rate Track and adjust their strategy accordingly to mitigate the spread of disease.

**Expert Tips:**

1. **Communicate with Your Team:** Share information about available resources, outbreak locations, and cure development progress.
2. **Focus on High-Priority Locations:** Target cities with high numbers of infected people or those critical for curing a specific disease.
3. **Develop Cures Strategically:** Prioritize research efforts based on the most pressing diseases and limited resource availability.

By understanding these rules and strategies, expert players can unlock the full potential of Pandemic, working together to save humanity from extinction.
