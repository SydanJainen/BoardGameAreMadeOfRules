# Rule Explanation for Adult Audience

### Wingspan Game Rules Overview

**Objective:** The goal of *Wingspan* is to accumulate the most points by collecting birds, gaining food tokens, laying eggs, achieving end-of-round goals, and tucking bonus cards. The player with the highest total score wins.

---

#### Components:
- **Bird Cards**: 170 unique bird cards representing various species.
- **Bonus Cards**: 26 bonus cards offering additional scoring opportunities.
- **Egg Miniatures**: 75 marbled egg miniatures in different colors.
- **Food Tokens**: 103 tokens (5 types: invertebrate, seed, fish, fruit, rodent).
- **Action Cubes**: 40 cubes of one color per player.
- **Goal Tiles**: 8 double-sided tiles for setting end-of-round goals.
- **Bird Tray and Birdfeeder Dice Tower**.
- **Player Mats**: One for each player to track their bird collection.
- **Scorepad**.
- **First Player Token**.

---

#### Setup:
1. **Shuffle the Bird Cards**: Place them next to a bird tray, then populate the tray with 3 face-up cards.
2. **Supply**: Place all food and egg tokens in the supply (accessible to all players).
3. **Birdfeeder Dice Tower**: Toss custom wooden dice into it.
4. **Goal Board**: Choose between Green or Blue side based on your preference for competition level.
5. **Shuffle Goal Tiles**: Place 1 tile randomly from each of the four spaces, with both sides visible.
6. **Bonus Cards**: Shuffle and place them on the table.

Each player:
- Receives a player mat, 8 action cubes, 2 random bonus cards, 5 random bird cards, and 5 food tokens (1 of each type).
- Keeps up to 5 bird cards, discarding others proportionally.
- Chooses 1 bonus card to keep.

Randomly select the first player and give them the token.

---

#### Gameplay:
**Turn Structure:**
On your turn, you can take one of four actions:

1. **Play a Bird from Your Hand:**
   - Select a bird card in hand.
   - Place an action cube at the top of the habitat column where you want to play the bird.
   - Pay food and egg costs (if any) by discarding corresponding tokens/cards.
   - Place the bird on your mat, triggering its "WHEN PLAYED" power if applicable.

2. **Gain Food and Activate Forest Bird Powers:**
   - Place an action cube in the leftmost slot of the "gain food" row.
   - Gain food from dice in the birdfeeder (discard a die for each type).
   - Optional bonus conversion to gain more food by discarding a card.
   - Activate brown powers on your forest birds.

3. **Lay Eggs and Activate Grassland Bird Powers:**
   - Place an action cube in the leftmost slot of the "lay eggs" row.
   - Lay eggs from the supply (place them on bird cards).
   - Optional bonus conversion to lay more eggs by discarding a food token.
   - Activate brown powers on your grassland birds.

4. **Draw Bird Cards and Activate Wetland Bird Powers:**
   - Place an action cube in the leftmost slot of the "draw cards" row.
   - Draw bird cards from either face-up trays or the deck (no hand limit).
   - Optional bonus conversion to draw more by discarding eggs.
   - Activate brown powers on your wetland birds.

**Managing the Birdfeeder:**
- The birdfeeder has a tray with 5 food dice. 
- Use these dice to gain food tokens, and remove used dice from the tray until refilled.

**Managing Food Tokens & Eggs:**
- No limits on how many tokens or eggs you can have.
- You can cache food tokens for later use during game end scoring.
- Egg tokens are public information; each bird has an egg limit (cannot exceed it).

---

#### End of Round:
1. **Remove Action Cubes**: Clear all action cubes from your mat.
2. **Score End-of-Round Goals** based on the chosen goal method (Green or Blue):
   - For Green: Players compare their totals and place cubes in 1st, 2nd, or 3rd places.
   - For Blue: Each player scores 1 point per item counted.
3. **Discard Face-Up Bird Cards**: Clear all face-up cards on the tray and replenish with new cards from the deck.
4. **Rotate First Player Token** clockwise to the next player.

---

#### Game End:
- After Round 4, tally points for:
  - Each bird card (printed value).
  - Bonus cards (printed value).
  - Eggs cached or laid.
  - Food tokens cached at game end.
- The player with the highest total score wins. In case of a tie, the player with the most unused food tokens is the winner.

---

#### Tips:
- **Early Game**: Focus on getting birds that help you gain more cards or food early.
- **Brown Powers**: Utilize brown powers to their fullest as they can significantly aid your strategy.

Enjoy exploring the avian world in *Wingspan* and build the best aviary!
