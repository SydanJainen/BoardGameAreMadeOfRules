# Pandemic - Rules Explanation (for Kids)

## What Is This Game About?

Pandemic is a team game where you and your friends work together to save the world from four nasty diseases! The diseases are spreading across the map, and your job is to find cures for all four of them before things get out of control. You either all win together or all lose together -- no fighting each other!

## How Many People Can Play?

2 to 4 players, and it takes about 45 minutes.

## Setting Up

1. Put the big board in the middle of the table. It shows a map of the world with cities connected by red lines.
2. Everyone gets a **Role card** -- this tells you what special power you have. Each role has a matching colored pawn.
3. Put all the pawns in **Atlanta** -- that is your home base. Place a Research Station there too.
4. Deal out **Player cards** (city cards with blue backs) to everyone:
   - 2 players: 4 cards each
   - 3 players: 3 cards each
   - 4 players: 2 cards each
5. Now spread the starting diseases: flip 9 Infection cards. The first 3 cities each get 3 colored disease cubes, the next 3 get 2 cubes each, and the last 3 get 1 cube each.
6. Mix some scary **Epidemic cards** into the Player deck (4 for an easy game, 5 for normal, 6 for hard).

## What You Do On Your Turn

Every turn has three steps:

### Step 1: Take 4 Actions

You get to do 4 things. Pick from these:

- **Drive/Ferry** -- Move your pawn to a city next door (connected by a red line).
- **Direct Flight** -- Play a city card from your hand and fly straight to that city.
- **Charter Flight** -- If you have the card that matches the city you are standing in, play it and fly anywhere on the map.
- **Shuttle Flight** -- If you are at a Research Station, hop to any other Research Station for free.
- **Build a Research Station** -- Play the card matching the city you are in and build a station there. You need these to cure diseases!
- **Treat Disease** -- Remove 1 disease cube from the city you are in. If the disease is already cured, you remove ALL cubes of that color at once!
- **Share Knowledge** -- If you and a teammate are in the same city, you can give or take the card that matches that city.
- **Discover a Cure** -- If you are at a Research Station and have 5 cards of the same color, discard them to cure that disease!

### Step 2: Draw 2 Cards

Pick up 2 cards from the Player Draw Pile. Watch out -- if you draw an **Epidemic card**, bad things happen! The diseases get worse and cities that were already sick can get sick again.

### Step 3: Infect Cities

Flip over some Infection cards (the number depends on the Infection Rate track) and add 1 disease cube to each city shown.

## Outbreaks -- When Things Go Wrong

If a city already has 3 cubes of one color and it would get another one, that is an **outbreak**! Instead of adding a 4th cube, you put 1 cube in every city connected to it. This can cause a chain reaction if those cities are full too!

## How You Win

Find cures for ALL FOUR diseases (blue, yellow, black, and red). You do not need to remove every cube -- just discover all four cures and you save the world!

## How You Lose

You lose if any of these happen:
- The **Outbreaks marker** reaches 8 -- too many outbreaks!
- You run out of disease cubes of any color -- the disease spread too far!
- You need to draw Player cards but the deck is empty -- you ran out of time!

## Special Roles

Each player has a unique power:
- **Medic** -- Removes ALL cubes of one color in a city with one action instead of just one cube.
- **Scientist** -- Only needs 4 cards (instead of 5) to discover a cure.
- **Researcher** -- Can give any card to another player, not just the card matching the city.
- **Operations Expert** -- Can build a Research Station without playing a card.
- **Dispatcher** -- Can move other players' pawns as if they were his own.

## Top Tips

- Talk to your teammates! Discuss your plans together.
- Focus on getting 5 cards of the same color to one player so they can cure a disease.
- Do not ignore outbreaks -- treat cities that have 3 cubes before they blow up.
- Use Research Stations to travel around the map quickly.

Good luck saving the world!
