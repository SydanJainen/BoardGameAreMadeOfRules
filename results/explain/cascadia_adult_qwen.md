# Rule Explanation for Adult Audience

Certainly! Here's a clear and concise explanation of the rules for Cascadia, designed for an adult audience:

### **Cascadia Game Rules Overview**

**Objective:** The objective is to create the most diverse Pacific Northwest environment by drafting Habitat Tiles and Wildlife Tokens to construct a beautiful landscape mosaic. Players compete to gain the highest score based on various scoring objectives.

#### **Setup:**
1. **Tile and Token Preparation:**
   - Place all 100 Wildlife Tokens in the Cloth Bag.
   - Randomly select the number of Habitat Tiles required for the number of players (43, 63, or 83 tiles) from the box and shuffle them face down.
   - Place any excluded tiles back into the box.

2. **Scoring Cards:**
   - Randomly select one Wildlife Scoring Card for each wildlife species (Bear, Elk, Salmon, Hawk, Fox).
   - Place these cards in a central area where all players can see them.
   - Return unused Wildlife Scoring Cards to the box.

3. **Starter Habitat Tiles:**
   - Each player receives one Starter Habitat Tile and places it face-up in front of them.
   - The remaining Habitat Tiles are returned to the box.

4. **Initial Play Area:**
   - Reveal 4 Habitat Tiles from the face-down stacks and place them face-up in the center of the play area.
   - Draw 4 Wildlife Tokens from the Cloth Bag and pair them, in order, with each of the 4 Habitat Tiles to form 4 combinations.

5. **Nature Tokens:**
   - Place Nature Tokens within easy reach of all players (typically 25 tokens).

6. **Starting Player:**
   - The player who most recently saw one of the wildlife in the game will be the first player, or you may select a starting player randomly.

#### **Gameplay:**
1. **Turn Sequence:**
   - Players take turns clockwise.
   - On each turn, a player selects one Habitat Tile and Wildlife Token combination from the center display (4 pairs).

2. **Handling Overpopulations:**
   - If 3 of the available Wildlife Tokens are the same, the active player may choose to wipe these tokens aside and draw new ones in their place.
   - If all 4 available Wildlife Tokens are the same, they are automatically wiped.

3. **Placing Tiles and Tokens:**
   - Place the selected Habitat Tile adjacent to an existing tile (Starter Tile or previously placed tiles) but not on top of another tile.
   - Place the Wildlife Token according to its matching wildlife type and adjacency rules:
     - Cannot place a token if no open habitat can support it.
     - Can place a token on any available habitat.
     - Getting a Nature Token from a Keystone Tile allows you to take an additional Habitat or Wildlife Token.

4. **Nature Tokens:**
   - Spend Nature Tokens for various actions (e.g., taking extra tiles, wiping tokens).
   - Unused Nature Tokens at the end of the game are worth 1 point each.

#### **End Game & Scoring:**
1. **Turns and End Condition:**
   - Each player plays exactly 20 turns.
   - The game ends when there are no more Habitat Tiles to replace those taken during a turn.

2. **Scoring:**
   - **Wildlife Scoring Cards:** Score points based on the Wildlife Scoring Cards for each wildlife type (Bears, Elk, Salmon, Hawks, Foxes).
   - **Habitat Tile Corridors:** Gain 1 point per habitat corridor of at least 3 tiles.
   - **Majority Scoring:** Bonus points for having the largest contiguous group in each habitat type.

3. **Ties:**
   - The player with the most points wins. In case of a tie, the player with more unused Nature Tokens wins. If still tied, share the victory.

#### **Variants and Achievements:**
- **Family Variant:** Use only the Family Variant Wildlife Scoring Card.
- **Intermediate Variant:** Similar to Family but uses Intermediate Wildlife Scoring Cards.
- **Solo Mode & Scenarios:** Follow a solo setup with specific objectives and scoring cards. Pass scenarios by completing their goals.

### **Conclusion:**
Cascadia is a strategic tile-laying game that challenges players to balance habitat creation, wildlife placement, and point-scoring strategies. The unique combinations of Habitat Tiles and Wildlife Tokens create dynamic gameplay opportunities for each turn. Enjoy crafting your Cascadian ecosystem!
