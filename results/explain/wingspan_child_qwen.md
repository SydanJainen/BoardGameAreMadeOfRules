# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

Sure! Let's imagine you're playing a fun bird collection game called Wingspan with your friends or family. Here’s how it works:

### Setting Up the Game

1. **Get Your Stuff Ready**: Each player gets a special mat where they will keep their birds, some action cubes (like little blocks), 2 bonus cards, and 5 bird cards.
   
2. **Bird Cards and Tokens**: Shuffle all the bird cards to make a big deck. Put 3 bird cards on your mat face-up. You also need food tokens (like tiny coins) and egg miniatures.

### Playing the Game

The game is played over 4 rounds, where you take turns doing different things:

#### Round 1: Play a Bird
- **Choose Your Action**: At the start of each turn, you decide what to do. You can:
  - Play a bird from your hand.
  - Gain food by rolling dice in the birdfeeder tower.
  - Lay eggs on birds.
  - Draw more bird cards.

#### Playing a Bird
1. **Pick a Bird**: Look at the bird card and see if you have enough food tokens to pay for it.
2. **Pay Food Tokens**: Put down as many food tokens from your mat as the cost of the bird says.
3. **Place the Bird**: Place the bird on your mat in one of the three habitats (forest, grassland, wetland).
4. **Use the Bird’s Power**: If the bird has a special power when it's played, use that now.

#### Gain Food
1. **Roll Dice**: Roll all the dice not in the birdfeeder.
2. **Pick Up Tokens**: Choose which food tokens to take based on what the dice show.
3. **Use Bonuses**: You can sometimes gain extra tokens by playing a card or using a power.

#### Lay Eggs
1. **Place an Egg**: Put an egg on any of your birds, following their limits.
2. **Use Bonuses**: Sometimes you can get more eggs with food tokens too!

#### Draw Bird Cards
1. **Choose Your Birds**: Pick up new bird cards from the tray or the deck to add to your collection.

### End of Round

- **Clean Up**: Take out all the action cubes and count how many points you earned.
- **Score Goals**: Place a cube on the goal board based on what you have (like having the most birds in one habitat).
- **Replace Cards**: Put back any face-up bird cards from your mat.

### The Winner

At the end of 4 rounds, the player with the most points wins! Points come from:
- Birds on your mat
- Bonus cards
- Eggs and food tokens
- Tucked cards (special bonus cards)

And that’s it! You’re now ready to start playing Wingspan and collecting the prettiest birds. Have fun!
