# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

Hey there, young adventurer! Are you ready to learn about the exciting game of Century?

Century is a fun board game where you play as a caravan leader, trying to collect points by trading spices, claiming points, and upgrading your caravan. Let's break down the rules in a super simple way!

**Setting Up the Game**

First, we need to set up the game. We have three types of cards: Point cards (which give us points), Merchant cards (which we can buy and trade with), and Spice cards (which let us collect spices).

We shuffle the Merchant cards and draw 5 cards to make our starting hand. Each player gets one 'Create 2' card and one 'Upgrade 2' card, which are special cards that help us.

**Taking Turns**

Each player takes a turn in a clockwise direction. When it's your turn, you can choose from four actions:

1. **Play**: Play a Spice card or a Merchant card to collect points or trade with other players.
2. **Acquire**: Buy a new Merchant card by placing one cube on each card to the left of it. You get to keep the cubes that were on those cards!
3. **Rest**: Put all your played cards back into your hand, like a reset button!
4. **Claim**: Take a Point card if you have all the right spices (cubes) in your caravan.

**Spice Cards and Trading**

When playing a Spice card, you collect the number and color of cubes shown on the card. For example, if it's a red cube, you get one red cube!

If you play a 'Trade' card, you can swap some cubes from your caravan with new ones from the supply.

**Upgrading**

When playing an 'Upgrade 2' card, you can upgrade a single cube in your caravan by one level. You can do this twice!

**Winning the Game**

The game ends when one player has taken five Point cards (or six in a three-player game). Then, we count all our points:

* Gold tokens are worth 3 points each
* Silver tokens are worth 1 point each
* Non-yellow cubes are worth 1 point each

The player with the most points WINS!

That's it! Century is a fun game where you get to collect spices, trade with friends, and upgrade your caravan. Are you ready to join the adventure?
