# Rule Explanation for Adult Audience

**Century: Spice Road - Game Rules**

**Components:**
- Point Cards (orange back)
- Merchant Cards (purple back)
- Caravan Cards (grey back)
- Gold Tokens
- Silver Tokens
- Spice Cubes (Turmeric, Saffron, Cardamom, Cinnamon)
- Point Cards Deck & Discard Pile
- Merchant Cards Deck & Display
- Caravan Cards in play
- Spice Bowl with colored cubes

**Setup:**
1. **Point Cards:** Shuffle the deck and place 5 cards face up on the table (the display). Place gold tokens (number of players x2) above the first card, and silver tokens (number of players x2) above the second.
2. **Merchant Cards:** Separate the starting cards with purple borders. Each player gets one 'Create 2' and one 'Upgrade 2'. Shuffle the remaining Merchant cards and place 6 face up on the table next to the deck.
3. **Spice Cubes:** Place cubes in the bowl according to color, matching the order: Yellow (Turmeric), Red (Saffron), Green (Cardamom), Brown (Cinnamon).
4. **Caravan Cards:** Deal one card to each player; the first player marker determines who goes first.
5. **Spice Allocation:** Place yellow and red cubes on players' Caravan cards based on their position:
   - 1st player: 3 Yellow
   - 2nd & 3rd players: 4 Yellow
   - 4th & 5th players: 3 Yellow, 1 Red

**Gameplay:**
Century is played over several rounds with each player taking one turn per round in a clockwise direction.

On your turn, perform **one** of the following actions:

*Play:*
- Play Merchant cards face up:
  - *Spice Cards:* Take cubes from the bowl and place them on your caravan.
  - *Upgrade Cards:* Upgrade cubes on your caravan by one level each (not mandatory). You can upgrade different cubes with the same card.
  - *Trade Cards:* Return specified cubes to the bowls, then take different cubes from the supply.

*Acquire:*
- Pay for a Merchant card by placing cubes on adjacent cards in the display. Take the acquired card into your hand. Replace it in the display and draw a new card from the deck.

*Rest:*
- Take all played cards back into your hand to build your Merchant card hand.

*Claim:*
- If you have required cubes, return them to the bowl, take the Point card face down, and collect gold/silver tokens if applicable. Replace it in the display and draw a new card from the deck.

**End of Turn:**
If you have more than 10 cubes on your caravan, return excess cubes to the bowls until you reach your limit.

**Game End:**
Once a player takes their fifth Point card (sixth in a 2-3 player game), finish the current round, and tally points:
- Each gold token = 3 points
- Each silver token = 1 point
- Non-yellow spice cubes on caravans = 1 point each

The player with the most points wins. In case of a tie, the last player to take a turn wins.
