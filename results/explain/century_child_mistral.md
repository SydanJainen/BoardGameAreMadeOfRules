# Rule Explanation for Child Audience

**Welcome to Century! Here's how we play:**

1. **Get Ready:**
   - Shuffle the orange cards (Point cards) and take 5, put them face up on the table.
   - Put some gold and silver coins near the first two Point cards.
   - Choose special Merchant cards for each player: one 'Create 2' and one 'Upgrade 2'. The rest go back in the box.
   - Shuffle other Merchant cards (purple backs) and take 6, put them face up next to their deck.
   - Put spice cubes (yellow, red, green, brown) into bowls like this: Yellow, Red, Green, Brown.
   - Give each player a Caravan card. The one with the star is first.
   - Put yellow and red cubes on each player's Caravan card as shown here:

   | Players | Yellow Cubes | Red Cubes |
   |---------|-------------|------------|
   | 1st     | 3           | 0          |
   | 2nd     | 4           | 0          |
   | 3rd     | 4           | 0          |
   | 4th     | 3           | 1          |
   | 5th     | 3           | 1          |

2. **Take a Turn:**
   Each round, everyone takes one turn. You can do one of these things:

   - **Play:** Use a card from your hand.
   - **Acquire:** Get a new Merchant card.
   - **Rest:** Take back cards you played earlier.
   - **Claim:** Take a Point card.

3. **Playing Cards:**
   - **Spice Cards:** Take colored cubes shown on the card and put them on your Caravan.
   - **Upgrade 2 Cards:** You can upgrade two cubes, one level each time. For example, yellow to red, then red to green.
   - **Trade Cards:** Give back some cubes and take new ones from the supply.

4. **Acquiring Cards:**
   To get a Merchant card, put one cube on each card before it, then take the card. The first card is free!

5. **Resting:**
   Take all cards you played face up back into your hand.

6. **Claiming Points:**
   If you have all cubes shown on a Point card, return them and take that card. You'll also get gold or silver coins sometimes.

7. **Caravan Limit:**
   Your Caravan can hold up to 10 cubes. If you have more, send some back to the bowls.

8. **Game Ends:**
   When someone takes their fifth Point card (sixth in a 2-3 player game), we finish the round and count points:
   - Each gold coin is worth 3 points.
   - Each silver coin is worth 1 point.
   - Each non-yellow cube is worth 1 point.
   The player with the most points wins!

**Now you know how to play Century! Let's start our adventure on the Spice Road.** 🌶️🗿
