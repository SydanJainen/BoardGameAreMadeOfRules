## Corrected Rules

**1. Action Cube Exhaustion:**
If a player runs out of action cubes, they cannot take any more actions in that round and must wait until the next round begins.

**2. Managing the Birdfeeder:**
- When multiple players are taking actions simultaneously, the birdfeeder tray is managed by the first player to complete their turn.
- If the birdfeeder tray is ever empty, all 5 dice are thrown back into the tray.

**3. Selecting the First Player:**
The first player can be selected using a die roll or drawing from a stack of tokens marked "First Player."

**4. Playing Bird Cards:**
If a player cannot afford to pay the full cost of a bird card, it must be discarded and replaced with another card.

**5. Consistent Processes for Gaining Food:**
- When gaining food, place an action cube in the leftmost exposed slot in the "gain food" row on your player mat.
- Remove dice from the birdfeeder tray to gain corresponding food tokens.
- If a bonus conversion is available, you may discard 1 bird card to gain additional food.

**6. Tying in End-of-Round Goal Scoring:**
- For goals using the Green method (1st, 2nd, and 3rd place), if players tie for any place, place both cubes on that place.
- At game end, add points for that place and the next place(s) and divide by the number of tied players, rounding down.

**7. Winning Conditions:**
- In case of a tie with no unused food tokens, players should share victory based on the highest total score from other scoring methods (birds, bonus cards, etc.).

**8. Brown Powers Definition:**
"Brown powers" are optional bird card abilities that can be activated when using the corresponding habitat.
